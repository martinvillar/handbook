Cuando partimos de una base común de valores compartidos, el resto son detalles de implementación. Esto segundo es muy importante, somos un grupo de gente que HACE cosas. No solamente pensamos, recomendamos y diseñamos, sino que nos arremangamos y hacemos.

First of all, we hired you because we consider you to share the same values and principles that matter to us - if you've detected any you highly disagree with, warn as soon! When we kick off a common base of shared values, everything else is about implementation details. Secondly, and para-quoting Joel Spolsky, we hired you because we think you're smart and can make things. That last thing is really important - we're a group of people who MAKE stuff. We don't just think, suggest and design - we put hands on and make.
